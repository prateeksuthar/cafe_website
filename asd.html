Our Story

Founded in 2010, Sunny Side Cafe began as a small coffee cart with a dream to serve exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming environment. Today, we're proud to be a beloved part of our community.

We source our beans directly from ethical growers and bake all our pastries fresh every morning. Our mission is simple: to make your day a little brighter with great coffee and good food.
